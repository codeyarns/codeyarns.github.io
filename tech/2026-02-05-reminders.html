Reminders is a todo app, or a task management app that works across MacOS and iOS, with syncing through iCloud.

  • Install:

The app was pre-installed on my MacOS. For iOS devices, it can be installed from the App Store here.

  • Online:

It can also be used through iCloud online here.

  • Tasks:

Click on any list and you can add tasks inside it. Optionally, a task can also be assigned reminder date/time, repeat cadence, and tags. Clicking the checkbox circle marks the task as completed.

  • Lists:

The kinds of lists that can be created are: standard, smart list and groceries. In addition to its name, a list can be given a color and an icon.

A list shows the un-completed tasks by default. Click on Show to see both completed and un-completed tasks. Click on Clear to remove the completed tasks.

  • Sections:

Sections can be added in a list to organize the tasks inside a list.
