Find read-write speeds of USB devices with USBDeview

📅 2012-Jul-28 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ usbdeview ⬩ 📚 Archive

Your computer has many USB ports and you use many USB storage devices by connecting them to these ports. You might be curious about the read-write speeds of your USB storage devices with these ports. Testing these speeds is easy with the USBDeview tool.

Connect your USB storage device, open USBDeview, right-click on the device, choose Speed Test and in the dialog choose Start Test. USBDeview will perform a sequential read-write test on the device over USB and report back the results.

Once the test is complete, you can also choose to share your speed report with everyone by publishing it automatically online. USB speeds reported by other people from their devices can be seen online here.

Tried with: USBDeview 2.11 and Windows 7
