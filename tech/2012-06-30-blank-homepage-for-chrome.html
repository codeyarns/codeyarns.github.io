Blank homepage for Chrome

📅 2012-Jun-30 ⬩ 📚 Archive

Whenever the Chrome browser is opened, it shows all your bookmarks, huge icons of all Google services and lots of other crap on the homepage.

If you want a simple blank homepage, go to Settings → On startup → Open a specific page or set of pages → Set pages and type in about:blank as the homepage URL.

Tried with: Google Chrome 20.0.1132.47
