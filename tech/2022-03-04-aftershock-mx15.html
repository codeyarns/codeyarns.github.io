I bought the Aftershock MX-15 gaming notebook in the February of 2017 in Singapore. I chose this wanting to have a powerful personal computer that I could use for C++ and CUDA development and for deep learning training. Back then it cost me S$1350.

Specifications

  • CPU: Intel i7-7700HW @ 2.8GHz
  • RAM: 8GB DDR4
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050
  • AC/DC Adapter: ADP-120MH D
  • AC power cord: BS 1363 (British/Singapore) to IEC 60320
  • ME FW version: 11.6.10.1196
  • Motherboard series: N85_N87,HJ,HJ1,HK1
  • BIOS version: 1.05.01TASP
  • KBC/EC Firmware revision: 1.05.01

Details

  • This notebook is actually a branded and customized version of the Clevo N850HJ notebook. In fact, it lists this model number at the bottom of the case.
  • No keyboard shortcuts are shown with the AfterShock boot screen. But, you can press F2 to get to the BIOS screen and F7 to get to the boot devices list.
