Rotation Control app for Android

📅 2015-Sep-21 ⬩ 📚 Archive

Rotation Control is an app for Android that makes it easy for you to set the rotation mode on your phone or tablet.

  • It shows all the supported rotation modes in notification area. This makes it very easy to choose or change a rotation mode.

  • If you want the home screen to also rotate then tap the Guard icon.

Tried with: Rotation Control 1.0, Android 5.0.2 and Moto G2 (XT1068)
