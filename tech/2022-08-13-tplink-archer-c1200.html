TP-Link Archer C1200 v3.0

📅 2022-Aug-13 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ wifi router ⬩ 📚 Archive

TP-Link Archer C1200 v3.0 is the wireless router I used at home from 2019 to 2023.

  • Wireless clients: Multiple computers, smartphones and media players are connected to our router over wifi.

  • Wired clients: There are 4 Ethernet ports at the back where computers can be directly connected to the router and be part of the same LAN as the wireless clients.

  • USB Disk: This router has a USB 3.0 port at the back to connect a Windows partitioned disk. The disk will be mounted and can be accessed as a Windows network share (\\TP-Share) or through FTP (ftp://router_ip_address:21).

  • Firmware: This has been updated multiple times over the years and is currently at 3.0.8 Build 20211219 Rel. 66975.
