How to edit contact in Android

Ashwin Nanjappa

2013-12-24 11:11:12+00:00

In Android, I typically call someone by opening the Dialer (also called Phone) and looking up the contact from the directory there. Strangely, you cannot edit any of the contact details from that app!

To edit the name, phone number or any other details of a contact, you need to open the People app and edit the details there.

Tried with: Android 4.3 and Moto G
