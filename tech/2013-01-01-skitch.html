Skitch

📅 2013-Jan-01 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ skitch, windows ⬩ 📚 Archive

Skitch is a nice little application to add annotations or simple graphics (arrows, boxes) to screenshots or photos. This is typically to share something funny with friends or share something technical with others. It is now available as a Windows 8 app on the Windows Store.

Tried with: Skitch 2.01128.6064 and Windows 8 Pro 64-bit
