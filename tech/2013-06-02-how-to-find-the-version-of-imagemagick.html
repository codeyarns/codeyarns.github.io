How to find the version of ImageMagick

2013-06-02 09:06:44+00:00

The ImageMagick tools do not have a --help parameter.

To find out the version of ImageMagick installed on your system, use the --version parameter:

$ convert --version

Tried with: ImageMagick 6.6.9-7 and Ubuntu 12.04 LTS
