Garmin Instinct 2S Solar

📅 2025-Sep-27 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ watch ⬩ 📚 Archive

Disappointed by the Apple Watch, I was looking for a smartwatch with longer battery life and a size that fits my small wrist and discovered Garmin Instinct 2S Solar. This watch turned out to have the softest, most comfortable strap I have ever tried and its closely spaced holes make it easy to have a great fit on the wrist.

Buttons

There are 5 buttons, 3 on the left and 2 on the right:

  • CTRL/Light: Single-press to toggle the backlight. Double-press to toggle the flashlight.
  • Menu/Up: Hold to view menu.
  • ABC/Down: Hold to view Altimeter, Barometer and Compass screen (ABC).
  • GPS:
  • Set/Back: Press to return to previous screen.

Glances

Press Up and Down buttons to scroll through the Glances. Info shown includes:

  • Solar intensity graph
  • Weather
  • Sunrise
  • Last Sport
  • Steps
  • Heart rate
  • Measure heart rate
  • Sleep
  • Calendar
  • Notifications

Watch faces

The watch ships with a few faces and many more can be found online and installed to the watch by using the ConnectIQ app on the iPhone.

  • Change watch face: Long press the Menu button and choose Watch Face. Scroll up and down through the various watch faces. Many of the watch faces have a Customize option that lets you customize the face. Some of the watch faces can be customized only through the ConnectIQ app.

  • Instinct Pro: This is a watch face that I like for this watch. It can be found and installed using the ConnectIQ app. Note that it can only be customized using the app. Documentation for the watch face can be found here.

Garmin Pay

  • Open Garmin Connect app, press on your watch (top right corner), press on >.
  • Choose Wallet & Garmin Pay and add a new card.

Usage

  • Disable heart rate monitor: Hold menu button to get to menu options. Choose Sensors & Accessories -> Wrist Heart Rate and change Status to off.
