Disappointed by the Apple Watch, I was looking for a smartwatch with longer battery life and a size that fits my small wrist and discovered Garmin Instinct 2S Solar.

Buttons

There are 5 buttons, 3 on the left and 2 on the right:

  • CTRL/Light: Single-press to toggle the backlight. Double-press to toggle the flashlight.
  • Menu/Up: Hold to view menu.
  • ABC/Down: Hold to view Altimeter, Barometer and Compass screen (ABC).
  • GPS:
  • Set/Back: Press to return to previous screen.

Glances

Press Up and Down buttons to scroll through the Glances. Info shown includes:

  • Solar intensity graph
  • Weather
  • Sunrise
  • Last Sport
  • Steps
  • Heart rate
  • Measure heart rate
  • Sleep
  • Calendar
  • Notifications

Garmin Pay

  • Open Garmin Connect app, press on your watch (top right corner), press on >.
  • Choose Wallet & Garmin Pay and add a new card.

Usage

  • Disable heart rate monitor: Hold menu button to get to menu options. Choose Sensors & Accessories -> Wrist Heart Rate and change Status to off.
