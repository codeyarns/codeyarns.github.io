Thinkpad USB-C Dock Gen 2

📅 2023-Mar-01 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ dock ⬩ 📚 Archive

I have been using this Thinkpad USB-C Dock Gen 2 from Lenovo for a few years now at my desk and with my Thinkpad X1 Yoga (Gen 4) notebook. This is a USB-C dock that can be used for charging a compatible laptop and also extend its display to 2 other DisplayPort displays and 1 HDMI display.

Specifications

  • Model number: LDC-G2
  • Input power (max): 90W
  • Output power over USB-C (max): 60W
