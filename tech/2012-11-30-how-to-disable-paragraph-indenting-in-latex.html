How to disable paragraph indenting in LaTeX

(First posted on: 2012-11-30 07:30:32+00:00)

By default, LaTeX indents the first line of a paragraph. If you find this irritating, it can be turned off by just including the package parskip:

\usepackage{parskip}

This disables the indenting and also adds a bit of vertical space between all paragraphs.

Tried with: MikTeX 2.9
