Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1 with S Pen

📅 2022-Dec-27 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ android, tablet ⬩ 📚 Archive

Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1 with S Pen (also known as SM-P580) is a 10.1-inch Android tablet I got a few years to try taking notes and sketching with a pen.

Specifications

  • Dimensions: 10.0” x 6.46” x 0.32”
  • Display: WUXGA (1920 x 1200), PLS LCD
  • Processor: Exynos 7870 Octa-Core(1.6GHz)
  • Battery: Li-Ion 7300mAh
  • Weight: 1.22 lb (553 g)
  • Memory: 3 GB RAM, 16 GB ROM
  • Front camera: 2.0 MP
  • Rear camera: 8.0MP AF with flash
  • Buttons: Power and volume up-down on the right, Home at the bottom

Tricks

  • Restart: The tablet may need to be restarted if it has run out of battery and is not responding. First charge the tablet with some power using the wall outlet power adapter. Once you think it has enough power to boot, press and hold the power and volume down buttons simultaneously for 7 seconds to boot up.

  • Factory reset: The factory reset option from inside the Android OS was time consuming, required my encryption PIN and Google account credentials. Instead, a factory reset from the Android recovery screen is more convenient. To do this, power off the tablet and then hold down 3 buttons: Power, Volume Up and Home. Let go when you see a Installing system update. It will change to No command and then finally go into the Android Recovery screen. Choose Wipe data/factory reset here and then choose Reboot system now to boot into a fresh tablet.
