Microsoft Teams is a communication tool for the workplace with features of chat, call and meetings with audio and video.

Keyboard shortcuts

Here are some keyboard shortcuts I like to use in the Meeting app:

  • Ctrl + Shift + M: Toggle mute and unmute.
  • Ctrl + Shift + O: Toggle camera on and off.
  • Ctrl + Shift + E: Share content.

Features

  • Noise suppression: This is a great feature that removes all non-speech sound originating at your end when you speak. To set this, click on your profile in top-right corner and Settings -> Devices -> Noise Suppression. The level is set to Auto by default, but with a noisy home I turn it to High, even though I have a headset with a noise-canceling mic.
