My life has truly been transformed by free/open-source software, free web services and public radio/podcasts/news. When I was a student in India and Singapore, I could not really afford to support them. From the past few years, I have been donating annually to the entities behind the services I use and I plan to continue to do every year. It is my belief that your donation might be small or big, it still matters since it shows real support for the creators and maintainers and lets them know that someone appreciates what they are doing.

A non-comprehensive list of who I donate to:

  • Calibre
  • Internet Archives
  • IrfanView
  • Mastodon
  • NPR/PBS
  • Wikipedia
