20,000 Hits!

2010-09-01 02:23:26+00:00

ChooruCode passed 20,000 hits today! :-)

I am happy to see that people from around the world are finding what is posted here useful. ChooruCode was, is and will remain my little logbook. This will remain the place where I record nuggets of learning that crop up while using programming languages, tools and software. I find it more useful to jot down and share them here than to maintain them in private text files, documents or Google Docs. The ease-of-use of Wordpress.com has played a major role in making this writing experience painless.

Almost all of the traffic to ChooruCode comes from Google search results. Most hits are during the weekdays and hits fall during weekends. The hit rate has been growing steadily and is currently at ~5000 hits/month.

Looking forward to the milestone of 50,000 hits now! :-D
