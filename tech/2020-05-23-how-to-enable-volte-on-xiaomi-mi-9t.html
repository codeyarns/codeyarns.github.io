How to enable VoLTE on Xiaomi Mi 9T

📅 2020-May-23 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 📚 Archive

Imagine my shock when my cellphone provider Ting emailed me warning that all the four US carriers are shutting down their voice networks in favor of VoLTE (Voice over LTE). The email said that my phone Xiaomi Mi 9T does not have VoLTE capability and so I should switch to a phone that does. This meant that I will not be able to make or receive voice calls soon.

It turns out that my phone does have VoLTE capability. Here is how to enable it:

  • Open the dialer app and dial this number to enable VoLTE feature: *#*#86583#*#*
  • Go to Settings -> SIM cards -> T-Mobile (or your provider). You should see a Use VoLTE option here. Enable it if it is not already enabled.
  • You should see a VoLTE icon appear in the status bar at the top of your phone beside the cellphone signal strength symbol.
