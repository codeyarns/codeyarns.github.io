Launchy

📅 2023-Jun-10 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ windows ⬩ 📚 Archive

Launchy is a classic program launcher for Windows.

  • Install: It can be downloaded and installed from here.

  • Launch: Once it is running in the background, it can launched anytime by pressing Alt + Space. This shortcut can be changed in its Options.

  • Scale to high DPI: I found that Launchy looked tiny on my high DPI Windows 10 system. To make it look a normal size, I opened the Properties on C:\Program Files (x86)\Launchy\Launchy.exe. Here I chose Compatibility -> Change DPI settings and under High DPI Scaling Override chose System.

Tried with: Launchy 2.5 and Windows 10 x64
