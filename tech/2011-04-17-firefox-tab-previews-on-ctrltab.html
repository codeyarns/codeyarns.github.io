Firefox: Tab Previews on Ctrl+Tab

Ashwin Nanjappa

2011-04-17 01:21:46+00:00

Firefox can display a Windows task-manager-like previews of open tabs when Ctrl+Tab is used to switch or cycle through the tabs. To enable this feature head over to about:config and set browser.ctrltab.previews to true.

Tried with: Firefox 4
