How to convert blog to PDF

📅 2014-Jan-28 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ blogspot, pdf, wordpress, wordsmith ⬩ 📚 Archive

There are some awesome blogs out there whose archives are worth reading like a book. One way to do this is to convert the blog into a PDF. You can do this for blogs hosted at Blogspot or Wordpress using the WordSmith.

Go to the WordSmith webpage and provide the URL of the blog. Choose whether you want the latest posts (up to a maximum of 100 posts) or provide a time range (up to a maximum of a year). At the end of the process, you get a nice little PDF ebook of the blog posts. Have fun reading! 😊
