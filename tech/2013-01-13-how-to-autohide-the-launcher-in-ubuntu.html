How to autohide the Launcher in Ubuntu

2013-01-13 04:18:45+00:00

The Launcher is the bar at the left which holds application icons. To autohide it, click the gear icon in the top right corner of the display. Choose System settings → Appearance → Behavior. Here you can find the settings to autohide the Launcher and the action to reveal it when necessary.

Tried with: Ubuntu 12.04.1 LTS
