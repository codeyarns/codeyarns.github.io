Outlook Cheatsheet

(First posted on: 2020-06-09T22:17:43.401874-07:00)

The Outlook Windows program (not OWA) can be a productive tool once you tweak its UI and learn the keyboard shortcuts for your common tasks.

  • Ctrl+1, Ctrl+2, Ctrl+3: Switch to Mail, Calendar, or Contacts view.
  • Ctrl+N: Create new email (when in Mail view).
  • Ctrl+R: Open reply email to sender.
  • Ctrl+Shift+R: Open reply email to sender and everyone CCed on original email.
  • Alt+S: Send the email.
  • Backspace: Archive the current email or conversation.
  • Esc: Close currently open email window or any window or dialog.
  • Ctrl+E or F3: Search emails.
  • Find in the current email: Open the email in a new window and then press F4.
  • Ctrl+Shift+B: Open address book to search for people.
  • Alt+Down: Switch to next message in conversation.
  • Alt+Up: Switch to previous message in conversation.

Reference: Outlook keyboard shortcuts
