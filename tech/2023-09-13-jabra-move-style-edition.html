Jabra Move Style Edition

📅 2023-Sep-13 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ headphones ⬩ 📚 Archive

The Jabra Move Style Edition is a pair of wireless headphones that I have used for many years now.

  • Very lightweight, comfortable padded earcups and a snug fit around the head. It is designed to be used during running or gym with a snug fit and soft materials.
  • The cups are on-ear, so they do not make them sweat while also not being inserted into the ear canal.
  • During Bluetooth pairing it appears with the device name Jabra MOVE v2.5.0.
  • It can be connected to 2 devices at the same time. Also, it can be paired with a total of 8 devices.
  • It can also be connected as wired headphones with an audio cord. Bluetooth is automatically disabled when connected this way.
  • This headphones has the most user-friendly buttons I’ve seen in any headset. The button on the right is for power on/off and pairing. It has a helpful spring action that is great haptic feedback. The button on the left is for volume. It has a clear high notch that makes it easy to find and also to feel and know if I’m increasing or decreasing volume. Perfect design!
  • Micro-USB for charging.
