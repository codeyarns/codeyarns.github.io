Anker Soundbuds Sport earphones

📅 2022-Dec-31 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ bluetooth, headphones ⬩ 📚 Archive

Anker Soundbuds Sport (A3233) is a pair of wireless bluetooth earphones that I got many years ago to use while running. (Anker is now fully focused on battery and charging products, but a few years ago they were into audio too.)

  • Appearance: Each of the earbuds has a rubber earhook to secure it within the ear. The earphones came with 3 sizes of eartips and earhooks. The earbuds are connected by a cable that rests behind the neck. The cable has a fithook that can be used to secure the cable so it does not interfere during physical activities.

  • Features: IPX4 water resistance and supported Bluetooth 4.0.

  • Buttons:

    • The right earphone has a power button. Long press to power on or go into pairing mode. Long press again to power off. Short press to play or pause.
    • The left earphone has volume up-down buttons.

  • Battery: The left earphone has a micro USB-C slot for charging. The earphones promise a 8-hour playtime on full charge.
