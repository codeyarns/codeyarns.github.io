Webcamoid is an app that I installed when I was trying out several virtual passthrough camera apps. Later when I found it lacking, I uninstalled it. But it would still appear as a virtual camera in video conferencing apps like MS Teams.

To fully remove it, I had to dig further:

  • Open the list of services in Windows and look for AkVCamAssistant or Webcamoid. Stop and delete this service.

  • Delete the C:\Program Files\AkVirtualCamera.plugin folder.
