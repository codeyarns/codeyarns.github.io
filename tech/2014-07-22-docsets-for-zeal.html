Docsets for Zeal

Ashwin Nanjappa

2014-07-22 03:58:28+00:00

Zeal can download all the docsets that are shared by Dash. However, docsets for many libraries are not yet available through Dash. Thankfully, many folks on the internet have created docsets for most of such libraries. These can be loaded into Zeal easily.
