WSL Cheatsheet

📅 2023-Dec-27 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ wsl ⬩ 📚 Archive

  • Ubuntu hangs on start: Apparently this is some weird error that occurs after a laptop resumes from sleep. We need to find the right svchost.exe, kill it and then shutdown WSL to fix this.
    • Find the PID of the svchost.exe we need: tasklist /svc /fi "imagename eq svchost.exe" | findstr LxssManager
    • Find this PID in the Details tab in Task Manager and kill its entire process tree.
    • Shutdown WSL: wsl.exe --shutdown.
