How to get a fortune greeting in every terminal session

2013-08-14 14:27:46+00:00

[caption id="attachment_3747" align="aligncenter" width="354"][](http://codeyarns.files.wordpress.com/2013/08/20130814-cowsayfortune.png) Cowsay-Fortune greeting[/caption]

This is probably the oldest trick in the book: getting a fun greeting every time you open a new terminal session. The typical way to do this to call fortune. If you combine it with the many ASCII cow and other animal characters available in cowsay, it becomes really cool.

One way to do this is to add this to your bashrc:

https://gist.github.com/ashwin/6231544

This works on Ubuntu. If you're on some other Linux, you may have to change the directory of the cow files.

Tried with: Ubuntu 12.04 LTS
