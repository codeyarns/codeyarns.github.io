I noticed that Player FM, which I used to listen to podcasts, had gotten naggy with ads and in-app purchases. Looking for an alternative I settled on AntennaPod.

  • Unlike Player FM, this app is fully open-source, with its source code available at https://github.com/AntennaPod.

  • The app is available both from F-Droid store and at the Google Play Store.

  • The UI is simple, allowing you to search and subscribe to podcasts. How many podcasts to download regularly and storage issues can be managed in the settings.

  • In the podcast playing UI the playback speed can be customized. This is an important feature for me since I like to listen at 1.5-2x speeds.

I am not an avid podcast listener, I just catch up on a handful of podcasts once in a while and I found that this simple podcast app fits my needs.

Tried with: AntennaPod 2.7.1
