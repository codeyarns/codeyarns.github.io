Redragon Kumara K552 keyboard

📅 2023-Mar-08 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ keyboard ⬩ 📚 Archive

The Redragon Kumara K552 is an inexpensive mechanical keyboard I use as a backup.

  • This is a 87-key tenkeyless 60% keyboard with red LED backlit keys.

  • The keyboard is plug and play, there are no drivers required.

  • There are no dip switches or anything similar under the keyboard to configure.

  • Press Fn + ScrLk to toggle backlight.

  • Press Fn + Home to toggle breathing light.

  • Press Fn + left Win if you find that Win and APP key are locked.

  • On USB, the keyboard appears as SINO WEALTH USB KEYBOARD.

    • Vendor ID: 0x258A (Sino Wealth Electronic Ltd.)
    • Product ID: 0x1006
