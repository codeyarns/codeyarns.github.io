How to split line in Vim

(First posted on: 2014-08-25 07:18:06+00:00)

To split a line at the cursor position you can use the usual sequence of i, Enter and Esc. This enters insert mode, adds a new line and then escapes from insert mode.

If your cursor is at a space in English text then you are in luck. You can insert a newline here by replacing the space with a newline character. To do that in Vim, press r and Enter. No need to escape insert mode!

Tried with: Vim 7.4
