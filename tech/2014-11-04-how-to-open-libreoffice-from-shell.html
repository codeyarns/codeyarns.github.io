How to open LibreOffice from shell

2014-11-04 08:23:50+00:00

The commands to open the programs of LibreOffice from shell:

  • Writer: libreoffice --writer

  • Calc: libreoffice --calc

  • Impress: libreoffice --impress

  • Database: libreoffice --base

  • Draw: libreoffice --draw

  • Math: libreoffice --math
