How to flip a matrix in Octave

(First posted on: 2014-05-12 05:06:43+00:00)

To flip a matrix about the horizontal axis (up to down), call the flipud function.

To flip a matrix about the vertical axis (left to right), call the fliplr function.

> A = [ 1 2 3; 4 5 6];
> A
A =
   1   2   3
   4   5   6
> flipud(A)
   4   5   6
   1   2   3

These functions are from Matlab and so work in it too.

Tried with: Octave 3.8.1 and Ubuntu 14.04
