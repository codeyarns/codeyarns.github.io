Evince

📅 2023-Oct-06 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ pdf viewer ⬩ 📚 Archive

Evince, also known as Document Viewer in Ubuntu, is the default PDF viewer in GNOME.

  • Highlight: Text can be annotated by marking it with the mouse and right-click choose Highlight Selected Text. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl + h.

  • Post-It Note: Add a Post-It note anywhere on the doc by pressing s and clicking the cursor where you want the note. A note pops up and any text can be typed into it.

Tried with: Evince 42.3 and Ubuntu 22.04
