LEd and MikTeX 2.9

📅 2011-Apr-26 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ latex, led, miktex ⬩ 📚 Archive

The LEd (LaTeX Editor) was last released in 2009 and supported MikTeX 2.8 back then. To use it with MikTeX 2.9, open Configuration → Options and change these settings:

  • Set TeX executables option to C:\Program Files\MiKTeX 2.9\miktex\bin (32-bit Windows) or C:\Program Files (x86)\MiKTeX 2.9\miktex\bin (64-bit Windows).

  • Leave the TeX distribution option at MikTeX 2.8

Tried with: LEd 0.53
