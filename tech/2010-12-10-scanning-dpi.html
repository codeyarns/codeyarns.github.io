Scanning DPI

📅 2010-Dec-10 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ dpi, scanning ⬩ 📚 Archive

A good legible DPI for scanning A4-size written or typed content:

For B&W: 200 DPI and above (150 DPI misses details in glyphs)

For Color: 300 DPI and above
