How to change citation style of BibLaTeX

📅 2013-Jan-20 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 📚 Archive

The citation style of BibLaTeX can be changed easily by changing its style parameter. The description and usage of various styles can be found in the BibLaTeX documentation.

For example, to change the citation style to only show author name and title of article:

%...

\usepackage[style=authortitle]{biblatex}

%...
