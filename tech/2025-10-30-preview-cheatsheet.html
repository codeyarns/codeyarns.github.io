Preview Cheatsheet

📅 2025-Oct-30 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ cheatsheet ⬩ 📚 Archive

Preview is the default application in MacOS to view images and PDF files.

PDF files

  • Highlight: Choose View -> Show Markup toolbar so that it is visible at the top. Mark the lines with the mouse cursor and choose the Highlight button in the Markup toolbar. Keyboard shortcut is to mark the lines and press control command h.
