Xiaomi Mi 9T

📅 2022-Oct-31 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ xiaomi ⬩ 📚 Archive

Xiaomi Mi 9T (model M1903F10G) is an Android smartphone that launched in and that I got in . It has served me well until now.

Specifications

  • Size: 156.7 x 74.3 x 8.8 mm
  • Weight: 191 g
  • Display: 1080 x 2340, 6.39 inches, AMOLED
  • SoC: Qualcomm SDM730 Snapdragon 730
    • Big-CPU: Kryo 470 Gold (2x Cortex-A76 @ 2.2GHz)
    • Little-CPU: Kryo 470 Silver (6x Cortex A-55 @ 1.8GHz)
    • GPU: Adreno 618
    • AI Processor: 4x Hexagon 688
    • Process: Samsung 8nm

Features

  • Digital Wellbeing: This is a great feature in Android and can be found under Settings. It shows how much time I am spending on the phone and on which apps. I can set bedtime mode (when to switch to DND and grayscale mode), focus mode (pause distracting apps and notifications) and per-time app time limits.

  • Second space: MIUI supports maintaining two users on their phones using this feature and this phone is no exception to that. I can maintain a second Google account in the second space. And files can be transferred back and forth between the two spaces.

Software versions

  • 2022-10-30
    • Android 10
    • MIUI Global 12.0.9
