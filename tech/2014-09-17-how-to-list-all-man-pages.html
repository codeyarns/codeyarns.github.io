How to list all man pages

2014-09-17 03:06:58+00:00

To list all man pages on your Linux system:

$ apropos .

When you vaguely remember the name of a man page, grep the results of the above command to find out what you need.

Tried with: Man-db 2.6.7.1-1 and Ubuntu 14.04
