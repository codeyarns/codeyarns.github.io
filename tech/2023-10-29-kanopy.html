Kanopy

📅 2023-Oct-29 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ streaming ⬩ 📚 Archive

Kanopy is a streaming service to watch movies and TV series using your local library membership. It is only supported if your local library supports its use. Only the content curated by your library is visible to you and you are allowed a limited number of credits/movies to watch in a month.

  • Registration: Registering for the service is simple and involves entering your library name and your library card number to create an account. Once the account is created, you can login using the account credentials.

  • Watch online: The Kanopy website is also used to browse and search through the content and watch it in the browser itself.

  • Watch on Android: Install the app the Play Store here. Login with your credentials and watch the content you want. I found that Kanopy syncs the content I was watching and my last position in the videos between all the apps and online website.

  • Watch on Android TV: Surprisingly, the Kanopy app is not available on the Play Store of Android TV when I tried to install it on my NVIDIA Shield! I downloaded the latest .apk file of its Android TV app from here to a USB flashdrive, inserted that on the Shield and used a file explorer app to install the file. The Kanopy app installed using this method works fine on my Shield with my TV. FYI I downloaded the latest version and the nodpi variant.
