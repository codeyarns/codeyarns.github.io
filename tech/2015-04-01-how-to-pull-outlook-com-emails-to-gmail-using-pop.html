How to pull Outlook.com emails to Gmail using POP

📅 2015-Apr-01 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ gmail, outlook.com, pop ⬩ 📚 Archive

Assuming that you have a Gmail account where you would like to view the emails from your Outlook.com account. Here are the steps to achieve that:

  • Go to your Outlook.com account. Go to Options -> Managing your account -> Connect devices and apps with POP. Enable POP access here.

  • If you have enabled 2FA for your Outlook.com account, then go here. In the App passwords section, create a new App password.

  • Go to Gmail -> Settings -> Accounts and Import -> Check mail from out accounts using POP3. Click Add a POP3 account you own.

  • Provide your Outlook.com email address. If you have 2FA enabled on Outlook.com, then provide the App password generated above. Else give your login password. The POP server is pop-mail.outlook.com and port is 995.

You should be able to suck your Outlook.com mails into Gmail now.
