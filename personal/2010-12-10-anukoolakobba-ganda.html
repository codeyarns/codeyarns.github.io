Anukoolakobba Ganda (ಅನುಕೂಲಕೊಬ್ಬ ಗಂಡ)

(First posted on: 2010-12-10 17:49:26+00:00)

"Bad movies build character" says one of my friends. I wished that were true as I tried to sit through Anukoolakobba Ganda (ಅನುಕೂಲಕೊಬ್ಬ ಗಂಡ), a 1991 Kannada comedy starring Raghavendra Rajkumar and Vidyashree. Raja (Raghavendra) owns a car repair shop and is in love with Radha (Vidya). Throw in a couple of cousins who lust for Radha and an angry dad and you have a typical Indian movie. There is no saving grace in this movie, it is incredibly boring, avoid it any cost! :-D
