Anukoolakobba Ganda (ಅನುಕೂಲಕೊಬ್ಬ ಗಂಡ)

📅 2010-Dec-10 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ kannada, movie ⬩ 📚 Archive

“Bad movies build character” says one of my friends. I wished that were true as I tried to sit through Anukoolakobba Ganda (ಅನುಕೂಲಕೊಬ್ಬ ಗಂಡ), a 1991 Kannada comedy starring Raghavendra Rajkumar and Vidyashree. Raja (Raghavendra) owns a car repair shop and is in love with Radha (Vidya). Throw in a couple of cousins who lust for Radha and an angry dad and you have a typical Indian movie. There is no saving grace in this movie, it is incredibly boring, avoid it any cost! 😁
