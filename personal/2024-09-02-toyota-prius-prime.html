We recently got a Toyota Prius Prime, 2024 XSE edition, for our family. This post is an attempt to note the various features, jargon and technologies associated with this vehicle.

  • Driving Modes: Different driving modes can be chosen using the Drive Mode select switch by the driverâ€™s right side. Supported modes are Normal, Eco, Sport and Custom.

Driving assist features

  • Lane Change Assist (LCA)

  • Lane Tracing Assist (LTA)

  • Lane Departure Alert (LDA)

  • Proactive Driving Assist (PDA)

  • Front Cross Traffic Alert (FCTA)

  • Road Sign Assist (RSA)

  • Dynamic Radar Cruise Control (DRCC)

  • Driver Monitor Camera (DMC)

  • Traffic Jam Assist (TJA)

  • Blind Spot Monitor (BSM): Indicates to the driver if there are vehicles in the blind spot and it is not safe to change lanes. The driver is informed by illuminating the indicators on the outside rear view mirrors and a buzzing sound. This feature uses radar sensors installed on the left/right corners of the rear bumper.

  • Lane Departure Alert (LDA)

  • Safe Exit Assist (SEA): Indicates to the driver if there are vehicles in the blind spot and it is not safe to open the door. When the driver opens a door, they are informed by illuminating the indicators on the outside rear view mirrors and a buzzing sound. This feature uses radar sensors installed on the left/right corners of the rear bumper.

  • Intuitive Parking Assist (IPA): Shows the proximity of wall/objects to the car when parking the car. This is displayed on the multimedia display and also with a buzzing sound. This feature uses sensors on the front corners and rear corners.

  • Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA): When backing out of a parking spot, this uses the rear camera display to warn if there are vehicles crossing from the sides at the back of the car. This feature uses the rear side sensors.

  • Rear Camera Detection (RCD): Detects pedestrians right behind the vehicle. Sounds a buzzer and displays on multimedia display. This feature uses the rear camera (not the sensors).

  • ParKing Support Brake (PKSB): My car does not have this feature.

  • Toyota Teammate Advanced Park: My car does not have this feature.

  • Pre-Collision System (PCS)

  • Electronically Controlled Brake (ECB)

  • Anti-lock Brake System (ABS)

  • Brake assist

  • Vehicle Stability Control (VSC)

  • Enhanced VSC (EVSC)

  • Traction Control (TRAC)

  • Active Cornering Assist (ACA)

  • Hill-start Assist Control

  • Electric Power Steering (EPS)

  • Secondary Collision Brake
