The Tao of Cal

All of Cal Newport’s pontification summarized in a short blog post. This was the perfect reading to chew on before the new year begins. It is just a 5 minute read.

  • Treat cognitive context shifts as “productivity poison.”

  • Move as much collaboration as possible out of chains of ad hoc, back and forth messaging and into something more structured.

  • Remove all apps from your phone where someone makes more money the more you use it.

  • Get comfortable with discipline (doing things that are hard in the moment but important in the long term).
