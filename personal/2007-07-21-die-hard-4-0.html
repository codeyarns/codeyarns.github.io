Die Hard 4.0

📅 2007-Jul-21 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ ⬩ 📚 Archive

Die Hard 4.0 (4/5) -- Forget the pretentious hacking stuff in the movie and you're sure to be entertained. Bruce Willis as McClane has made a superb comeback along with Justin Long (the Apple guy). Hackers have closed down all of America's systems and it is upto old school McClane (a Timex watch in a digital age) and his techie young friend to save the world. McClane's jokes are great fun and man the action is great. None of that slow motion, stop action stuff. Go watch on the big screen!
