The Karate Kid

📅 2007-Jul-03 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ movie ⬩ 📚 Archive

The Karate Kid (4/5) – A classic fun martial arts movie where a Japanese handyman (Mr. Miyagi) helps a boy learn karate using unusual techniques (waxing his car) and helps him beat his bullies at school. The success of this movie spawned the entire Karate Kid sequels.
