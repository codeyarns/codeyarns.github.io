Scary Movie 3

📅 2005-Jan-31 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ movie ⬩ 📚 Archive

Rating: 2/4 (Decent watch)

Scary Movie 3 is the third installment of the mindless Scary Movie parody series. The movies juiced up in this one are Signs, The Ring and 8 Mile. To be honest, this movie was more fun than the very stupid Scary Movie 2. The way the three original movie storylines have been combined is nice. Decent watch.
