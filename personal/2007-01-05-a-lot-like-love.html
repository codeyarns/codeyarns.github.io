A Lot Like Love

📅 2007-Jan-05 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ ⬩ 📚 Archive

A Lot Like Love is supposedly a romantic movie. Emily (Amanda Peet) and Oliver (Ashton Kutcher) meet at an airport and make out during their flight. Oliver talks of a future where he wants to achieve some goals and then find the girl of his dreams. Emily mocks this. So they bet on it and decide to meet again 6 years later. Fatefully they keep running into each other every other year as they go through several relationships and career ups and downs. You don't need me to tell you that 6 years later all ends well and they find that they were meant for each other.

Amanda Peet is just okay but Ashton Kutcher just can't do anything but grin. The movie is boring and the pace is g-l-a-c-i-a-l. Don't watch.
