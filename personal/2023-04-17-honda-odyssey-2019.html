Notes about my 2019 Honda Odyssey minivan:

  • Automatic window rolldown: On a couple of mornings I had discovered that all 4 windows slightly rolled down! I had suspected foul play until an online search confirmed that this was caused by the car fob unlock button being held pressed for a few seconds. I tried it and found that after being held pressed for a few seconds, all 4 windows start rolling down until the key is held pressed.

  • Wireless remote battery: The wireless keyfob uses the CR2032 cell battery. This can be changed easily at home and does not require any visit to the service center.
