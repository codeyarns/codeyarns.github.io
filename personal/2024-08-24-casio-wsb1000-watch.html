The Casio WSB1000 is a new series of fitness digital watches (not G-Shock) that have Bluetooth connectivity. I got the blue colored WSB1000-2AV, but I do not recommend getting the white colored WSB1000-8BV (which looks better) because its negative display is unreadable in all lighting conditions.

  • Module: 3564

  • Size: 46.2 Ã— 41.3 Ã— 11.6 mm. It looks skinny and long. A bit of an odd look, but it fits my small wrist.

  • Weight: 36 g. It is weightless.

  • Battery: CR2016 that lasts 2 years. I would buy a solar powered variant of this watch if they made one.

  • Display: When displaying the time, the top row shows day of the week, the middle row the time and the bottom row shows year-month-date. There is a circular graphical display area in the top-right corner. It shows the countdown of 60 seconds at each minute during time mode and shows progress during step count display mode. There are 2 tiny markers to the left of this graphical display that remain both on when you are sedentary and they alternate when walking.

  • Buttons: This watch has 5 buttons, 1 in each of 4 corners and a special button at the bottom.
