Death Note

📅 2007-Jul-17 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ movie ⬩ 📚 Archive

Death Note (3/5) – The first of the 2 movies based on the popular Japanese manga series. It explores the face-off between detective L and Light who can kill people by just writing their name in a book. I saw the Japanese version with English subtitles. Not great, but not bad at all.
