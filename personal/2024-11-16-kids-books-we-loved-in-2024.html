Books loved by our little daughter

She turned 3 this year and started going to preschool. The books she loved or we loved reading to her:

  • Where Three Oceans Meet by Rajani Larocca. Beautifully illustrated by Archana Sreenivasan. An American girl of South Indian origin traces a journey from Bangalore to Kanyakumari with her mom and grandmom.

  • Dr. Seuss’s People, Places, and Things.
