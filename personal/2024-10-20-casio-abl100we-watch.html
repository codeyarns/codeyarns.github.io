Casio launched ABL-100WE watches in silver and gold colors. These are the updated Bluetooth versions of their vintage watches. I got the silver version (specifically Casio ABL100WE-1AVT).

  • **Case size*: 41.6 x 37.9 x 8.2 mm

  • Weight: 60 g. Feels like a feather, though it is a meta watch.

  • Battery: 2 years of battery life. CR2016.

  • Material: Resin case with chrome plated metal band.

  • Light: Press the top-right button for the super-retro amber backlight.

  • Strap: The length of the metal strap can be adjusted using a tiny screwdriver. Wedge it in the gap and pull up the tab, move it to where you want and press down.

  • Module: 3565

  • Bluetooth: Open the Casio Watches app on your phone, start adding this watch by entering the module number. Press and hold the bottom-left button on the watch, until it gets to the connecting mode and connects to the app. All the watch settings can be done conveniently from the phone after that. And of course, the watch will connect to the phone a few times a day to keep its time perfectly synchronized.

  • Step tracker: This seems to be the same step tracker UI that was in my Casio WSB1000 fitness watch.
