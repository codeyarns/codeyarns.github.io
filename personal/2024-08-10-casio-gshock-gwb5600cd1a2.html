The Casio G-Shock GWB5600 series bring Tough Solar, Bluetooth and Multi-band 6 radio to the regular G-Shock watches. There are 3 special watches in this series celebrating the Charles Darwin Foundation. I tried the GWB5600CD1A2, which has the hammerhead shark on the case and in the background when the light is used.

  • Size: 48.9 Ã— 42.8 Ã— 13.4 mm. Slightly larger than the regular G-Shock.
  • Weight: 53 g. Same as regular G-Shock.

I did not really like this watch because it has a slightly bigger curvature for its strap. That is, it makes a larger circle around the wrist and that cannot be tightened due to the larger supports provided under the watch. This means that it appears much looser on a tiny wrist like mine compared to the regular G-Shock. The strap is also wider. I am not sure why they did these changes, but probably because these special Charles Darwin watches use bio-based resin and maybe those require more support to endure the regular wear and tear of a G-Shock?
