SlimFold Micro Tyvek Wallet

📅 2023-Apr-29 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ fashion ⬩ 📚 Archive

Disgusted by the thickness and weight of a typical men’s wallet in the back pocket I had switched to an extremely slim card case as my wallet in Singapore. After many years that case fell apart and I looked for a similar wallet in the US and settled on the SlimFold Micro Tyvek.

Pros

  • Thinnest wallet in the world! 2mm thickness - there is no possible way any wallet can be thinner than this.

  • Almost zero weight! Literally nothing.

  • All the SlimFold wallets are made from Tyvek. This is a paper-like stiff synthetic material made by Dupont. In reality it feels like thin but metallic paper. This is an extremely strong and durable material - it has remained the same after many years.

  • The construction is solid. The stitches have held even after many years.

  • There are 2 slots outside and 2 slots hidden inside for credit cards. This is plenty for me.

  • There is also a space in the middle for currency notes.

  • Comes in fancy bright colors and patterns.

Cons

  • Biggest issue I have is that this Tyvek material does not have any grip on the cards. It is literally like being held between sheets of paper or thin metal - no grip! Though it was a bit snug in the beginning, after a while the cards just fall out. I have started to store the cards in the inner pockets just to avoid the chance of them falling out unnoticed.

  • The exterior color and surface fades and wrinkles like used paper after using for a few months. While a fade looks great on genuine leather, it does not look that great on Tyvek.

  • There is no design that makes this look or feel like (fake) leather.
