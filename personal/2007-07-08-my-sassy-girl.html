My Sassy Girl (엽기적인 그녀)

📅 2007-Jul-08 ⬩ ✍️ Ashwin Nanjappa ⬩ 🏷️ jun ji-hyun, korean ⬩ 📚 Archive

Rating: 3/4 (Cute Jun Ji-hyun domineers over this funny movie)

My Sassy Girl (엽기적인 그녀) is easily the most popular Korean movie I have heard about. It is a romantic comedy about a meek guy who falls in love with a sassy girl. The girl is cheery on the surface, but has some pain underneath. She takes it out on the guy by always domineering over him, slapping him and such. Several such incidents force them to breakup. Will they get back together? Jun Ji-hyun (from Il Mare) is cute and the movie is quite funny.
